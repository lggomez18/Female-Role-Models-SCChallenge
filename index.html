Eufrosina dislike how women where treated and expected to be in her community, therefore learned Spanish to later attend college. Even though she studied accounting, her passion was in the education system. She believed that through education she would make a change in her community.

In 2007, she challenged her community traditions and customs and ran for mayor. Her victory was not granted, but nothing stopped Eufrosina from continue with her vision and mission for her community, as shown in the documentaty "La Revolucion de Los Alcatraces".
